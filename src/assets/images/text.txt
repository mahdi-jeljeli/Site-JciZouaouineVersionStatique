Who We Are

We are JCI Zouaouine, a youth organization
that was founded in2019 and is affiliated with 
the global network of young leaders
known as Junior Chamber International (JCI).
We are more than just an organization;
we are a dynamic force, an evolving story of
resilience, and a testament to the transformative 
power of leadership.

WHAT WE DO

We offer diverse platforms for growth 
and impact, aligned with JCI’s Four Areas 
of Opportunity: 

• Individual Development,
• Community Action, 
• Business and Entrepreneurship, 
• InternationalCooperation.

Whether you want to start your own business,
improve your skills, volunteer for a cause,
or travel abroad, we have something for you.
